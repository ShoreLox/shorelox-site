About ShoreLox

ShoreLox is a locally owned and operated locksmith company proudly serving Atlantic County and Cape May County, New Jersey. We are fully licensed, bonded and insured and committed to providing fast, friendly and professional locksmith services at competitive rates. Whether you're locked out of your home, need to secure your business, or require emergency automotive assistance, our expert technicians are available around the clock to help.

Our Services

Residential

Lock installation & repair, rekeying, deadbolt upgrades, smart locks, key duplication and more to keep your home safe and secure.

Commercial

Master key systems, high‑security locks, access control, lock maintenance and emergency lockout services for businesses of all sizes.

Automotive

Car lockouts, key fob programming, transponder keys, ignition repair, broken key extraction and on‑site key cutting for most makes and models.

24/7 Emergency

Locked out late at night? We offer round‑the‑clock emergency locksmith services, with rapid response times throughout Atlantic and Cape May counties.
